War Rules

  • Each player picks 1 weapon per round
  • Player with strongest weapon is awarded 1 point
  • If similar weapons are chosen the round result is a tie (no points awarded)
  • Weapons ranking
    1. Rock beats Scissors
    2. Scissors beats Paper
    3. Paper beats Rock
  • First to reach 5 points win
